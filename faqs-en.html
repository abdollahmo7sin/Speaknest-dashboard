Frequently Asked Questions
Are the classes suitable for young children?
Yes, all our curricula are designed in an interactive way based on games and simulation to simplify complex information, making them ideal for children aged 6 years and above, with specialized tracks for adults as well.
What subjects do you offer?
We offer a wide range of subjects including English, French, Mathematics, and Sciences, all taught by professional teachers specialized in their fields.
How can I book a session?
You can easily book a session through your dashboard by checking the available schedule of your preferred teacher and selecting a suitable time slot.
